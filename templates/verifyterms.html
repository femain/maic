Provider Terms and Conditions

Providers agree to Healthier terms by registering and using their accounts. Providers must inform Healthier if they are exiting the service or they lose their bond. Any Change to the following Guidelines will be communicated via registered Email to Service Providers or updated in the Guidelines that can be viewed on the Healthier portal. Service Providers must have a dedicated service track solely for Healthier customers that is different from that of regular customers to guarantee immediate service delivery for on-time Healthier users. Service Providers have the obligation to respect their commitment, both in terms of price and service features offered. Healthier reserves the right to apply sanctions to providers that default on offered services. Healthier aim to pay providers immediately services are ordered, providers should expect payments for services within 24 hours of services being confirmed. Providers should send confirmation via Healthier mail to confirm service appointment. Payments would be reconciled every month end. Payments may be made on the next working day, if service booking occurred on holiday. A Service Provider that has been deactivated more than 2 times will be deleted on the Healthier platform.
