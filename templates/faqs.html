Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

What is HEALTHIER? HEALTHIER is a personalized mobile health manager. Healthier help identify healthcare needs and the best service providers for those needs. Why should I use HEALTHIER? HEALTHIER automates Health coverage processes, saving verification time for enrollees in accessing care, preparation time of bills, Receipts and encounter data for hospital staff and claims management time for HMO staff. Which platforms are supported? Web and Mobile platforms like Android and iOS are supported How do i use the Healthier platform to improve my Experience?
